Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location.

Can I return items?

Yes! You can return items within 30 days of purchase as long as they are unworn and in original condition.

How can I track my order?

You’ll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking details once your package ships.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping fees and delivery times vary by destination.

What sizes do you carry?

We offer sizes XS to XXL. Please refer to our size guide for exact measurements.

How do I apply a promo code?

Enter your code in the “Promo Code” field at checkout before placing your order.

Can I change or cancel my order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, please contact customer support as soon as possible.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! We offer digital gift cards in various amounts, sent instantly by email.
